Help

Registering a profile

  1. Click "Register" on the top-right
  2. Input your desired username and password and click "Register", you will be redirected to a page where you provide additional information
  3. Provide your information, upload a profile picture and then click "Register"
Congratulations, you now have a profile!

Edit profile

  1. First, you need to be signed in
  2. Press "More" at the top right of the page to open a drop down menu
  3. Press "Settings" to enter a profile settings page
  4. Edit the info you want changed and press "Apply changes"
Congratulations, you have changed your profile settings!

Logging in

  1. Click "Sign In" on the top-right
  2. Input your username and password and click "Sign In", you will be redirected to your feed
Congratulations, you are now logged in! If you want to log out, just press "Sign Out" at the top right of the website.

Uploading pictures

  1. First, you need to be signed in
  2. Press "Upload" at the top of the page to go to the upload page
  3. Press the "Browse..." button to choose an image and if you have an album you can choose to add the picture to it
  4. Press the green button "Upload" below your picture
Congratulations, you have uploaded your first picture!

Creating albums

  1. First, you need to be signed in
  2. Press "Upload" at the top of the page to go to the upload page
  3. Input the name of the album in the field "Album name"
  4. Press the green button "Create album" below
Congratulations, you have created your first album! If you want to add pictures to your album, press the "How do I upload pictures?" button above.
